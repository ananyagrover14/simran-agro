About Simran Agro

Simran Agro stands as a cornerstone in the rice industry, boasting a rich heritage spanning over five decades. Our story originates from the visionary ideals of Mr. Ujagar Singh in 1972, when M/S Phulka Rice Mill was established with an unwavering dedication to excellence and a profound love for rice. In 1982, Mr. Lal Singh, son of Mr. Ujagar Singh, joined the family enterprise, leading to the rebranding of the rice mill as M/S Guru Nanak Industries. Under his foresight, we ventured into exporting top-quality rice from 1991 to 1995. In 2000, the firm underwent another transformation, becoming M/S Simran Agro, as Mr. Gurpreet Singh, grandson of Mr. Ujagar Singh and the current proprietor, joined the family business.

Mission:

"Honoring Tradition, Pursuing Excellence: Our Commitment to Quality Rice Production"

Our aim is to emerge as a premier manufacturer of rice products while staying committed to quality and sustainability. We strive to deliver premium rice products to consumers, honoring the tradition of excellence and trust set forth by our founders.

What We Do:

"From Paddy to Plate: Delivering Premium Rice Products with Passion and Precision"

We specialize in rice shelling, operating on a contractual basis with the government. As per the agreement, the state government allocates paddy to our mill. Our responsibility involves shelling the paddy and supplying the resultant rice to the government. In return, we receive milling charges along with the by-products of paddy and rice, including rice bran and husk, which we market openly.

Our Process

Ensuring Excellence from Field to Table

Selective Sourcing

Carefully selecting the finest quality paddy from trusted local sources to ensure superior raw material.

Efficient Shelling

Utilizing advanced machinery like the Pneumatic Paddy De-Husker to efficiently remove husks while preserving grain integrity.

Precise Separation:

Employing the Paddy Separator to gently separate brown rice and husked paddy grains from shelled rice, ensuring purity.

Uniform Polishing

Using the Rice Cone Polisher for uniform polishing, minimizing breakage and maximizing efficiency in the process.

Stringent Quality Control

Meticulously inspecting each batch throughout the processing journey to maintain strict quality standards.

Advanced Sorting and Packaging

Utilizing optical sorting technologies to ensure only the highest quality rice reaches our customers' tables.
